The Tech Mahindra Foundation is the corporate social responsibility (CSR) arm of Tech Mahindra Limited, a Mahindra Group Company. The foundation was established in 2006 and has extensively worked in the fields of education, employability, and disability, with a focus on corporate volunteering. It has established itself as a prominent force for corporate social responsibility within the Mahindra Group. Inspired by this Spirit of Rise, the Foundation’s programs and initiatives are driving positive change in the lives of our communities with more than 150+ partners across 11 locations. The CSR vision of Tech Mahindra Limited is ‘Empowerment through Education’, implemented through Tech Mahindra Foundation’s programs in employability, education, and disability. The Foundation's key focus has been ensuring equal opportunities for women and persons with disabilities.

Enabling children to be purposefully engaged, youth to be constructively employed and equal opportunities for persons with disabilities.

We want you to Rise. And not just you. Your family. Your neighbourhood. Your whole community. This is why we come to work every day.

Celebrating each moment is about finding a higher sense of purpose, happiness, and achievement in all that we do.

Empowering all to Rise is about becoming a better version of ourselves each day.

Our Areas of Work

Employability

Skills-for-Market Training (SMART) is the Foundation's flagship employability program to empower youth from under-served urban communities with promising career opportunities. This program has been able to successfully bridge the gap between the demand and supply in various manufacturing and service industries.

Volunteering

Tech Mahindra associates actively engage with communities through the foundation, participating in a wide range of activities.More

Tech Mahindra associates actively engage with communities through the foundation, participating in a wide range of activities. These include teaching young children in municipal schools, mentoring teenagers preparing for competitive exams, organising blood donation drives, participating in sports meets, tree plantation drives Swachhata Hi Seva (cleanliness campaign), bicycle rallies, and celebrating special days with beneficiaries of partner organisations. The Foundation is dedicated to creating opportunities for every associate to contribute their share of corporate volunteering, to live up to the philosophy of 'Rise'.

One for All and All for One

Individual social responsibility (ISR) is an integral driving force for both the Foundation and Tech Mahindra at large. We encourage all of our associates to engage with their communities through the Tech Mahindra Foundation, partner NGOs, and directly-implemented projects that directly drive positive change while simultaneously enhancing their capacities for empathy and compassion. Our associates volunteer individually and in groups to ensure continuity, innovation, and to serve the greater good while they work.
Tech Mahindra associates are involved in a wide variety of activities. These include teaching young children in municipal schools, mentoring teenagers who are preparing for competitive exams; organising blood donation drives; participating in sports meets; tree plantation drives; Swachhata Hi Seva; and bicycle rallies to celebrate special days with beneficiaries of partner organisations.
